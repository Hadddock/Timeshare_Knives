OUR MISSION

Have you ever been holding something that you could cut but didn't have a knife and embarassed yourself in front of the whole school including Jenny Malone?

Not anymore buster. Us here at Timeshare Knives have got your back. We believe everyone needs knives, but obviously not all the time. So why pay for a knife when you could not be using it?

Enter knife timeshares. You buy into one of our three deluxe plans, and you gain access to a set of knives for a set period of time. When the time's up, you just clean off the knives and drive it over to the next person on your shared plan somewhere else in the continental US. Simple. Easy. Fun. Roadtrips.
